Error:
/lib64/libpangoft2-1.0.so.0: undefined symbol: FcWeightToOpenType 

Cause:
Fedora 22 use more recent version of fontconfig (2.11.94) than the one provided by Anaconda (2.11.1)

Fix:
conda install -c asmeurer pango
